Free Amenities

Breakfast

Enjoy the best breakfast at any of our hotels. We aim to provide you with practicality as well as save time. All this, at a much lower cost than any other non-affiliated hotel or even bakeries and restaurants.

Exercise/workout room

Take advantage of our gyms to exercise during your time at the hotel. We have personalized classes as well as equipment where you can do your own workout. We have free trial classes and special services with the best cost benefit.

Indoor pool

You can enjoy our swimming pools and changing rooms. We have pools for adults and pools for children.

WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs

Take advantage of free wifi throughout the hotel's facilities. Some pay TV channels are also available in the rooms and areas of the hotel where TV is available.

Parking

We offer a quality and safety service during your trip. Choose from the valet parking service or the car rental service for the time you are on the road.

Temple shuttle

We offer free shuttle service from hotel to temple and from temple to hotel again. The times are organized according to the opening hours of the temples.

Family history consultation and family ordinance card service

A service offered for free, which aims to bring you greater opportunities to grow your family tree. Our services will give you the opportunity to do research not only in Family History Centers, but also in our spaces at any of the hotels you choose to stay.

Specialized Services

Playroom

We offer a monitored playroom where you can leave your children while you serve in the temple or attend a meeting where they cannot attend. Our opening hours for this service are from 7am to 8pm. The service has a low cost, with a difference in values only on weekends and holidays.

babysitting

Our babysitting service is 24 hours, your children can stay in the games room and continue with the babysitting at the hotel after closing time. Costs vary depending on how long the service is needed.

Kitchenette suites

We offer full-time temple missionaries long-term accommodation in kitchen suites at a low cost.

Wedding reception hall

We offer a hall and buffet for weddings. It is necessary to schedule in advance. Check our prices for Saturdays and holidays.

Health

The service at our outpatient clinic is free and 24 hours a day. You will only pay for visits where you require specific medication, such as prescription drugs, previously indicated by your doctor. Simple emergency situations, such as minor injuries, malaise, among others, will be treated free of charge.
